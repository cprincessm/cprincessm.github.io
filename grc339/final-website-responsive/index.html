Former First Lady Michelle Obama is known for many things. Her latest book Becoming details all of her struggles and triumphs throughout her life as a Black woman up to this point. Praise from her book has led to a Netflix documentary under the same name and an upcoming podcast series. The popularity of her book isn't just hype, it's truly a good read.
