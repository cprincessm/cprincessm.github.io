Welcome to Royaltea Cafe!

Royaltea Cafe is your go-to place for all things tea and sweets. We offer an array of baked goods, ranging from cookies to pastries. To pair with these delicious sweets, we also have a wide range of teas that can be enjoyed hot or cold with specialty flavors. We also serve coffee.

We respect your safety and encourage you to order ahead of time. You can order by phone, online, or through a food delivery service.
