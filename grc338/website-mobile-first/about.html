Our History

As a result of the pandemic, many of us lost our main sources of income and had to pivot. Our passion for high-quality tea and baked goods pushed us to share our beloved hobby with others to bring joy to their days. We hope that our delicious items are able to brighten your day in any way possible.

We have many policies and procedures in place to ensure your safety as well as the safety of our employees.
